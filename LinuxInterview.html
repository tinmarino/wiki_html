Inspect

  • Memory
    • cat /proc/meminfo, vmstat, top, htop
    • free -m to display output in MB
    • free -g to display output in GB
  • Port
    • Parallel: (LPT) /dev/lp0, /dev/lp1, or /dev/lp2
    • Serial: /dev/ttyS0 to /dev/ttyS7 (COM in windows)
